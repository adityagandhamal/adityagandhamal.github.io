I'm a Research Fellow at [Sarvam AI](https://www.sarvam.ai/), where I work on large-scale data for Vision-Language Models. I was previously a Pre-Doctoral Researcher at the [Artificial Intelligence and Robotics Laboratory, Indian Institute of Science](https://labs.dese.iisc.ac.in/airlabs/), under the guidance of [Dr. Suresh Sundaram](https://dese.iisc.ac.in/staff/dr-suresh-sundaram/).

My research spans **Vision-Language Models**, **open-vocabulary segmentation**, **multimodal alignment**, and **Indic document intelligence**. I build systems that make VLMs more capable, robust, and adaptable across languages, modalities, and domains.

## What I'm working on

At Sarvam AI, I contributed to [**Sarvam Vision**](https://www.sarvam.ai/) — India's first sovereign Vision-Language Model (3B parameters, 22 Indian languages) — achieving state-of-the-art on olmOCR-Bench and OmniDocBench, outperforming Gemini 3 Pro, Mistral OCR 3, and GPT 5.2. I led large-scale data generation (150TB+, 260M+ images), evaluation pipelines, and an applied research project for the Government of India (MoSPI) on geospatial urban survey.

At IISc, I worked on adapting CLIP for open-vocabulary semantic segmentation using Optimal Transport, Masked Image Modelling, and parameter-efficient fine-tuning strategies. Our research resulted in 2 papers at **CVPR Workshops 2025**, with additional work under review at IROS 2026, TMLR, and IEEE TAI.
